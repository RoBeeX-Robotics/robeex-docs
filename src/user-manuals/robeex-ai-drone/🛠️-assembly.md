## Propeller Installation

Before flight, make sure that each **propeller** is installed on the **correct motor**, matching its **spin direction** (CW or CCW).  

::: warning
The **CW and CCW** propellers must be mounted on their compatible motors, matching the direction of spin for proper operation.
:::
::: warning
Ensure the propeller hub is fully seated on the motor shaft until the end of the shaft is visible (or flush) with the top of the hub.
:::


::: tip
Follow the **arrow directions shown on the RoBeeX main board near the rubber dampers** to identify each motorâ€™s rotation direction.
:::
