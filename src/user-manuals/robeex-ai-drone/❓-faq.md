# RoBeeX AI Drone - Frequently Asked Questions

## Flight Operations

### 1. What are the basic flight controls for RoBeeX?
RoBeeX can be controlled via smartphone app using virtual joysticks or gesture controls. Basic movements include:
- Left joystick: Ascend/Descend, Rotate
- Right joystick: Forward/Backward, Left/Right
- Auto-takeoff and Auto-landing with single tap

### 2. What is the maximum flight time and range?
- Flight Time: Up to 15 minutes per battery charge
- Control Range: Up to 100 meters in open areas
- Wi-Fi Range: 80 meters for live video streaming

### 3. How do I perform an emergency stop?
Press and hold the Emergency Stop button in the app, or flip the physical switch on the drone to immediately stop all motors and land.

## Safety & Regulations

### 4. Where can I safely fly RoBeeX?
- Fly in open spaces away from people, animals, and obstacles
- Avoid flying near airports, government buildings, or restricted areas
- Maximum altitude: 120 meters (varies by local regulations)
- Keep drone within visual line of sight at all times

### 5. What weather conditions are safe for flying?
- Wind speed: Below 8 m/s (18 mph)
- Temperature: 0°C to 40°C (32°F to 104°F)
- Avoid rain, snow, and fog conditions
- Do not fly during thunderstorms

### 6. How do I register my drone?
Check local aviation authority requirements. In many countries, drones over 250g require registration. RoBeeX weighs 280g with battery.

## Battery & Charging

### 7. How do I properly charge the battery?
- Use only the provided USB-C charger
- Charging time: Approximately 90 minutes
- LED indicators show charging status (red=charging, green=fully charged)
- Never leave battery charging unattended

### 8. How should I store the battery when not in use?
- Store at 30-50% charge level
- Keep in cool, dry place (15-25°C)
- Do not expose to direct sunlight or extreme temperatures
- Charge at least once every 3 months for long-term storage

## Camera & AI Features

### 9. How do I activate the AI tracking?
- Draw a box around the subject in the app
- Use voice command "RoBeeX, track me"
- Enable auto-follow mode for continuous tracking
- AI can track people, animals, and vehicles

### 10. What video and photo quality does RoBeeX support?
- Video: 4K/30fps, 1080p/60fps
- Photos: 12MP still images
- Supports HDR and low-light enhancement
- Electronic Image Stabilization (EIS) for smooth footage

### 11. How do I access my photos and videos?
- Download directly to your smartphone via Wi-Fi
- Use microSD card (supports up to 128GB)
- Cloud backup available with RoBeeX Plus subscription

## Technical Issues

### 12. What should I do if the drone won't connect?
1. Restart the drone and mobile app
2. Check Wi-Fi settings and connect to "RoBeeX-XXXX"
3. Ensure Bluetooth and location services are enabled
4. Update to latest firmware version

### 13. How do I update the firmware?
- Connect to stable Wi-Fi
- Open RoBeeX app and go to Settings > Firmware Update
- Keep drone powered on during update (10-15 minutes)
- Do not disconnect during the process

### 14. The drone is drifting during hover. What should I do?
- Perform IMU calibration in open space
- Check propeller condition for damage
- Ensure GPS has strong signal (8+ satellites)
- Calibrate compass if prompted by app

## Maintenance & Care

### 15. How do I clean and maintain my drone?
- Use soft, dry cloth to clean body and camera lens
- Check propellers for cracks before each flight
- Clean motor shafts with compressed air
- Store in provided carrying case when not in use

### 16. When should I replace propellers?
- Visible cracks, chips, or deformation
- After any crash or hard landing
- If drone shows vibration during flight
- Recommended to replace in full sets

## Advanced Features

### 17. How does the obstacle avoidance system work?
- Uses front and bottom vision sensors
- Automatic braking when obstacles detected
- Can be disabled for expert mode
- Works best in good lighting conditions

### 18. What smart flight modes are available?
- Orbit: Circling around a subject
- Waypoint: Pre-planned flight path
- Boomerang: Automatic out-and-back recording
- Panorama: 360-degree photo capture

### 19. Can I fly multiple RoBeeX drones together?
Yes, with Swarm Mode (advanced feature):
- Connect up to 5 drones in synchronized flight
- Requires additional swarm controller unit
- Advanced license may be required in some regions

### 20. What warranty and support does RoBeeX offer?
- Standard 1-year manufacturer warranty
- Free technical support via email and chat
- Extended warranty available for purchase
- Video tutorials and community forum available
