This Blockly program is designed to instruct a drone to perform the following actions:

* **Initial Ascent:** The drone will first fly up and maintain a stable altitude of **150 cm** (centimeters).
* **Face Tracking:** Once at the target altitude, the drone will continuously attempt to track any human face it detects.
* **Centering Mechanism:** To keep the detected face within the **center** of its image frame, the drone uses **yaw** movement (rotation around its vertical axis) to adjust its orientation.

---

### Program Summary

| Parameter | Value/Action | Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Initial Altitude** | 150 cm | Achieve operating height. |
| **Primary Task** | Face Tracking | Identify and follow a human face. |
| **Movement Used** | Yaw | Rotational adjustment to keep the face centered horizontally. |
